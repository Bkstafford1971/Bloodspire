Installation Instructions

  1. Download: Click the button above to download Bloodspire_Installer.exe.
  2. Security: If Windows SmartScreen warns you, select "More Info" and then "Run Anyway".
  3. Setup: Run the installer and follow the prompts to choose your destination folder.
  4. Connect: Ensure you have an active internet connection for turn synchronization.
  5. Battle: Launch the game and prepare your gladiators for the next weekend run.
Orders must be submitted by Friday at 9:00 PM to ensure your teams fight in the weekend arena.
