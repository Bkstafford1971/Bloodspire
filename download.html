Installation & First Launch

Getting started with BLOODSPIRE is simple. The official installer automates the setup so you can begin your ascent immediately.

What the Installer Does

  • Creates the game directory automatically on your system.
  • Places all required game files and assets into the directory.
  • Generates a Bloodspire shortcut on your Desktop for quick access.

Instructions

Download the BloodspireArena.exe and run it. The installer will create the necessary folders and place a shortcut on your desktop automatically. Once the process completes, launch the game via the desktop icon.

League Connectivity & Support
