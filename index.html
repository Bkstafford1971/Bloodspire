Welcome to Bloodspire, a text-based gladiatorial simulation where every decision could be your last. Manage your stable of warriors, train them in the dark arts of combat, and send them into the pit to win glory—or die trying.

Download & Installation

Download Bloodspire v1.0 (.exe)

Installation Instructions

1. Download the installer above.
2. Run the executable and follow the on-screen prompts.
3. Launch the game and connect to the server to register your manager account.

Generic Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11
  • Processor: 1.0 GHz or faster
  • Memory: 512 MB RAM
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
