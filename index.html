Welcome to Bloodspire

A bloodthirsty crowd roars as the gladiators enter the arena. Nervous but hardheaded team managers watch from their battle crested pavilions. Will months of training and patiently rehearsed strategy pay off with an arena victory? Or will all be lost on the jagged blade of an opponent's sword?

Apart, on the throne of honor, the arena Champion looks on, a body of knotted muscles and scars, a testimony to his own brutal rise. His visage is impassive but his thoughts boil. How long before some young warrior's sword finds his heart, perhaps one of those he now watches? He shakes himself, casting aside dark thoughts.

For the moment, he is the Champion, the best of the arena. His smoldering eyes scan the bloodstained sand where the warriors assume their fighting stances. He raises his clenched fist.

The gladiators leap forward. Victory, disgrace, or death awaits on the open sand.
